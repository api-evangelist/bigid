AI Access Governance is the practice of discovering and governing what AI systems can access across enterprise environments. Organizations often lack visibility into permissions AI inherits through applications, APIs, and service accounts, creating significant security risks. The approach helps identify excessive access and sensitive data exposure by connecting AI activities to the specific data they can reach.
