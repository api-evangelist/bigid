AI systems require permissions to operate effectively, yet most organizations lack visibility into what access their AI tools possess. AI typically inherits permissions through applications, APIs, service accounts, machine identities, and user roles, creating governance challenges when systems gain excessive access beyond their operational needs. BigID's platform helps organizations discover AI systems, analyze permissions, and connect access levels to sensitive data exposure to reduce risk.
