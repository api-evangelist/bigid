As organizations increasingly deploy AI agents that access applications and sensitive data, they must establish identity governance frameworks to manage these systems like enterprise identities. AI agents inherit permissions through existing infrastructure and introduce risks including unknown deployments, excessive access, ownership gaps, and data exposure. BigID's platform helps organizations discover, inventory, and govern AI identities to reduce operational security risks.
