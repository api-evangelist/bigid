Organizations can identify and remediate risks from excessive access permissions and outdated ownership records by monitoring five key risk categories including AI agents with broad permissions and orphaned identities. Permission sprawl and ownership gaps accumulate silently without continuous monitoring, making periodic audits insufficient for modern enterprise environments. The article advocates shifting to real-time detection and remediation powered by platforms that correlate identity context with data sensitivity.
